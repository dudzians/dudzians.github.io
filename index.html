AI Agent Development – Personal Project

This site describes a personal AI agent development project by Noah Dudziak. The project focuses on building and testing autonomous agents that can interact with users via SMS and other communication channels.

How Messaging Works

SMS messages are only sent to users who explicitly request access and provide their phone number. These messages are conversational responses, alerts, or results requested by the user.

No cold outreach. No marketing campaigns.

Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Example Messages

  • "Your AI agent found new results for your request. Reply for details."
  • "Task completed. Want a summary?"

Project Purpose

This is a hobby and research project used to experiment with AI automation, messaging workflows, and agent-based systems.
